The Goal

Sprout is a habit tracker for people who are looking to improve their own well being but need more encouragement in their lives. As you continue to improve a tree is bonsai tree is grown and managed by you! If you do not keep up with your daily tasks your tree begins to grow wilder eventually wilting from lack of care. Sprout keeps an optimistic light to your day and allows you to create something that is uniquely yours, helping you achieve your goals, create new things, and finds a way to help you improve.

Easy Startup and Usability

Even if you don’t know exactly what you want to accomplish with Sprout, it allows you to start with preset habits to edit and delete as you refine what you need to work on.

Encouraging Dialogue

Sprout helps you keep on track with your goals by using encouraging phrases and dialogue in order to push you to do your best.

Flexible Goals and Objectives

Sprout helps you keep track of your personal goals and set customised goals to fit with how you wish to improve yourself.

Is this a magic app?

No. Sprout does not believe in magic, you do need to work for what you need and want to improve. We cannot just give it to you.

How easy is it to keep up with my tree?

Not that easy, Sprout is kind and accommodating, but the app will not hold back. If you miss a day we will not hold back in terms of negative feedback in order to help you achieve your goals.
